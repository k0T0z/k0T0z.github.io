This blog post is related to my GSoC 2023 Project.

My finals started on 30 May 2023 and will be ended on 14 June 2023. I can't really focus on two tasks at the same time, so I decided to decrease my pace in the Steam project by:

  • Perparing the PR.
  • Joining the Steamworks group on Steam. Later on, I found out that it's for Steam partners only.
  • Reading the Steamworks documentation.

I started looking into these wrappers before I start coding Facepunch.Steamworks & steamworks4j, then I started to enhance the General API I implemented before. Something funny happened, I used the same naming convention as Steam SDK does, with the header guards also. The include was correct, but the compiler was unable to read SteamAPI_Init(); & SteamAPI_Shutdown(); functions. That's because I defined the header before Steam SDK does, so those functions were not defined yet. Waste of time!
